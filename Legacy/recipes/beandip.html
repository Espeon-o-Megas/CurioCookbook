Cow Eyeball Spicy Dip

Warning! This recipe is not for the faint of heart!

Look at you, little daredevil, choosing the recipe with the highest lethality rate (59%) in this entire cookbook! That takes some balls, we'll give you that! Worry not, for we will not dissapoint your morbid curiosity. After one bite of this diabolical dip you'll be crying for more! Literally!

"Shopping" List:

Here's all the stuff you'll need to make this hell of a sauce.

  • "Aged" Roma Tomatoes
  • Virgin blood (Scorpio sign works best)
  • Freshly severed cow skulls
  • Carolina Reapers (the normal kind)
  • Tabasco classic (0.5l)

Dirty Business:

This is what you'll do to actually prepare this shit.

  1. Crush the tomatoes in a blender until they form a chunky sauce.
  2. Mix the tomato chunks with the virgin blood and tabasco until it forms a cohesive mix. We're looking for a watery-smoothie consistency here.
  3. Place your newly mixed blend into a stainless steel sauce pan and reduce under low heat until it can cling to the back of a spoon and/or a spon dragged through the bottom leaves a clean trail.
  4. A bit before the sauce reaches the desired consistency, toss in the reapers and let them simmer for 30 seconds. This will give your sauce the pop! it needs!
  5. Finally, once thee sauce has thickened gently lower the gouged-out cow eyes into the mix, turn off the heat and let them cook until they turn milky white. Make sure to take your pan off the stove immediately after , as the eyes will continue to cook from the residual heat!

And with that you're done! Let this bad boy cool for a bit and then dive in into the gritty, spicy mix of your nightmares!
